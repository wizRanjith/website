Projects

Magizhagam is a 2bhk Residential house covering an built up area of about 1160 sq ft. It’s a single unit (Ground Floor) built at Perambur an old area of G.C.C (Greater Chennai Corporation). The owner’s wish was to build a Spacious & Ventilated house with Minimal usage of elements in both interior as well as exterior. The house falls under the broad category of Japandi / Scandinavian style in terms of the color palette where white plays a predominant role. Also, the house has some interesting architectural aspects such as long Wooden Windows, French Door, Skylight, Circle Wall and Tulsi Maadam to name a few.
